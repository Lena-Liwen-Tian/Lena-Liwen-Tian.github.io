Portfolio Dynamic and the Supply of Safe Securities [Internet Appendix]

QCGBF Young Economist Prize, Finalist
FMCG Best Paper Award in Banking/Financial Institution

By committing to maintain portfolio quality, institutions can create more safe debt, but are obligated to replace deteriorated collateral assets. This paper analyzes such dynamic collateral management in an equilibrium framework that endogenizes institutions' assets, liabilities, and secondary market trades.

Fund Flows in the Shadow of Stock Trading Regulation (with Xiang Kang)

Best Paper Award, 16th Conference on Asia-Pacific Financial Markets

Shutting down stock trading does not stop investors from producing information - at least when transformed liquidity is provided by financial intermediaries. Mutual fund investors do react to mispriced fund shares when underlying stock holdings experience suspension.
